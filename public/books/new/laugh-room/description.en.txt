Book Description
So Jung-ae's bestselling Strange Room Stories series
After irritation, tears, and jealousy, we arrive in the Room of Laughter!

Kang-i's grandfather has a tightly shut Room of Laughter!
Where could the key be?

After a traffic accident, Kang-i gains a strange ability. People's faces appear in different colors depending on their state of mind. And Kang-i's grandfather, who is always stern? Oh no, his face is a deep gray!
Everyone is said to have a Room of Laughter inside their heart. When something joyful happens, laughter blooms until the room is full. But Grandfather's Room of Laughter seems to have closed tight. No matter how funny things get, it will not budge. Kang-i feels both curious and frustrated. If Kang-i finds the key to the Room of Laughter, will Grandfather be able to laugh out loud too?

Author Bio
Written by So Jung-ae
Laugh Room is the author's 210th book. She has been loved by many readers and has received numerous literary awards. Always cheerful, she lifts the mood of people around her. A literary museum has even been built in her honor. Many people visit the So Jung-ae Literary Museum in Cheonan to spend pleasant time with the author. She writes and illustrates picture books herself and also writes entertaining full-length children's stories, sharing joyful days with her readers. Her major works include Irritation Room, Crying Room, Jealousy Room, The Suspicious Travel Friend, and Mom Is a Meerkat.

Illustrated by Bang Sae-mi
She works as a freelance illustrator and runs Saeang Books. She has published Woomi's Story, The Story of Two Witches, and The Elephant That Returned to the Forest.
